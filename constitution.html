I. Overview


BSFB is a true franchise building experience designed to mimic the NBA as close as possible.

Establishment
Scoring System is Head-to-Head Total Points: Teams compete Head to Head to earn a single Win, Loss or Tie each week based on which team scores the most Fantasy Points in a scoring period. All 30 NBA Franchises and playes will be represented. Full 82 Game Schedule for 12 teams with 8 teams making the playoffs and realistic year round off-season activity.

Formation
Our League began circa 2007-08 in the dorms of Arizona State University in Tempe (The same dorms that housed future NBA All-Star James Harden). This October we will be entering our 8th season as a league. Below is our "League Constitution", where all league rules can be found.

A. Structure
League Type: Head to Head Points
Regular Season Matchups: 14 Weeks
Weekly Player Limit: 40


2. Teams

Our leauge will have a maximum 12 teams each season. When filling the leauge preference will be given to who paid the leauge fee first and then secondly to veteren managers who are always welcome back.

Full Leauge History /and statsicial information can be found here.

III. Schedule
A. Regular Season

From November to March each manager will have ___ matchups over ___ weeks.

1. Protected Picks

Managers from the previous seasons have until August 1st to sign up for the leauge to retain their draft pick number. The following seasons draft order is based on the standings from the season before.

(Example: finishehing in 5th place would give that manager the #5 Pick next year.)



2. Draft

Leauge draft as traditionally been the last Sunday (night) in October. Sometimes that can be in November depending on the year. The goal is to have the draft the last weekend before the regular season begins.

Location: Online ESPN Draft Room

Rounds 1-8: 120 seconds per pick (90 seconds after rounds 9+)

2. Roster Dumping

Roster Dumping: When a team is out of playoff contention and drops the best players on their roster. Or also on the final day of a matchup (usually in playoffs) when a team drops majority of its roster to obtain a win. This is known as "roster dumping" and can be a rare occurance or even be done unintentionally.

It just needs to be said that this is extremely frowned upon in our leauge to drop a large portion(or best players) of your roster and obstructing the waiver wire.



IV. Players
A. Waivers

There is No Daily Waiver Wire Limit or Waiver Order.

Players can be added to teams at anytime during the season however a player added is not available until the following day to count.



C. Player Limit

Each team can start a max of 40 players each week. Going over that limit before or after the week is finished will result in the HIGHEST scoring player(s) from the final day being SUBTRACTED from your total score of that week. It is up to the managers to field exactly their best 40 to avoid the penalty.

If a player is placed in a roster spot in your lineup but does NOT play any minutes, then that player will NOT be added to your teamâ€™s weekly total.

* Also if for some reason ESPN decides to count a player put into your active roster towards your total AND that player did not enter the game at any point, please message a commissioner so we can edit the teams weekly total.

The Weekly Player Total can be viewed by clicking the "Full Box Score" button inside the matchup window on the "scoreboard tab".

There is no automatic block for playing over 40 players from ESPN. It will let you go over the player limit by default, howevre one of us will check the weekly totals on Sunday nights after the completetion of all games and adjust the scores if a/any manager has gone over the limit. All scoring adjustments are then posted to the leauge page the following day for complete transparency.

B. Trades

Trading Draft Picks:
Managers are allowed to trade draft picks once they are officially signed up for that current season. ALL draft pick trading must be done prior to the first pick of the draft. (Our leauge currently does not allow trading of future draft picks.)

1.Trade Votes / Veto
Managers are allowed to trade players immediately after the draft has concluded. Our league trade deadline has always been March 1st (two weeks after the official NBA trade deadline.) Managers are encouraged to post on the leauge Facebook Page with who is on their "trading block" and/or any postion they are looking for specifically in a trade.

Rules:
Once a trade has been accpected by both teams the changes will not take embed effect for 48 hours(if approved byt the league). A league commssioner will alsovpost the trade on the league page with voting options. Managers then have 2 days to vote to "accept" or "veto" the trade before it is offically completed.

ALL trades must have less than 5 votes from other managers after 2 days of it being accepted for it to be confirmed and processed.

In most cases trades will be approved. However if the trade is unblanced and provides a substanstial advantage to a team, then it is up to the other owners in the league to veto the trade and maintain league integrity.


Any managers with questions about specific rules plase do not hesiitate to message a commisioner or post on the facebook page.
