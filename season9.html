To Join/Sign up for next season:

1. Pay Leauge Fee -

Leauge Fee for Season 9 is $50.00.

Paypal or mail accepted message a commissioner when ready.

Protected Draft Pick Deadline: August 1st

Season 9 Sign-Up Deadline: September 1st

2. Accept Invite/Create Team -

Once payment is received you will recieve and EPSN invite via your ESPN Account email.

Only the Email associated with your current "ESPN account" will work please create an account if needed.

If for some reason the email invite is not working please message a commissioner.

3. Choose Team Name -

After accepting the invite from the "Edit Team" tab you can update team details and choose a name.

Note: *'s in team name may only be used to note championship(s).

4. Select a Team Logo -

Shown below are the exsisting logo templates avialable to choose from for Season 9.

Please message (Ben) when you know what logo and team colors you would like and I will add/update the logo.

5. Join FB Group -

Our Leauge Facebook group is where the leauge talks about trade chat / banter / rules.

Managers are encoureged to join to stay updated on the leauge.
